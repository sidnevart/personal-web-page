# Portfolio Site

Use this skill for public site writing and editing.

## Required reads

1. `portfolio-site.skill.md`
2. `global-market-positioning.skill.md`
3. `resources.md`
4. `CONTENT_GAPS.md` when a stronger claim or better visual is blocked by missing material

## Workflow

1. Reduce the page to the shortest copy that still sells the work.
2. Translate internal language into external business language.
3. Prefer product names a business person can understand over repository names.
4. Keep project descriptions serious, concrete, and outcome-led.
5. After drafting, run the final wording through the global market-positioning rules.

## Rules

- No GPT-looking filler.
- No empty “I am passionate” language.
- No fake metrics.
- Avoid long explanatory comparisons and meta-commentary.
- Prefer clear headlines, short summaries, and one strong idea per block.
