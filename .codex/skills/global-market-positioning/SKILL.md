# Global Market Positioning

Use this skill as the mandatory final pass on any public-facing text in this repository.

## Required reads

1. `global-market-positioning.skill.md`
2. The draft text or files just edited
3. `resources.md` if a wording choice depends on exact evidence

## What this pass should do

- Replace internal jargon with external market language.
- Remove hype, filler, and weak verbs.
- Make the positioning legible to ATS, recruiters, hiring managers, and founders.
- Keep the text calm, specific, and commercially useful.

## Rules

- Do not add new unsupported claims.
- Prefer clarity over cleverness.
- Prefer contribution and result over tool lists.
- If a sentence sounds inflated, generic, or robotic, rewrite it.
